APEX Legend Klassenübersicht

ASSAULT

Combat Utility & Initiation
  • Breaking enemy shields increases sprint and reload speeds.
  • Carry extra Ammo and Ordnance. Access red Weapon Supply Bins.
  • Automatically reload stowed weapons after a short delay.
Legenden: Bangalore, Revenant, Mad Maggie, Fuse, Ballistic

SKIRMISHER

Combat Mobility & Escape
  • Reveal Care Package contents from a distance.
  • Allies get a speed boost when running towards you at long range.
  • Gain health and shield regen on a knock.
Legenden: Wraith, Horizon, Mirage, Valkyrie, Pathfinder, Octane, Ash

RECON

Enemy Intel & Tracking
  • Scan Survey Beacons to reveal nearby enemy positions for a short time.
  • Gain threat vision when aiming down sights.
Legenden: Bloodhound, Seer, Vantage, Crypto, Sparrow

SUPPORT

Team Survival & Supply
  • Access hidden loot in blue Extended Supply Bins.
  • Faster revives (unless by D.O.C.) and HP regen after revives.
  • Banner crafting spawns Mobile Respawn Beacons.
Legenden: Lifeline, Loba, Newcastle, Wattson, Mirage

CONTROLLER

Area Setup & Control
  • Activate Ring Consoles to reveal the next circle's location.
  • Second activation allows EVAC from Ring Console.
  • Gain 25 shield and slow regen in safe zone.
Legenden: Caustic, Wattson, Rampart, Catalyst, Conduit
